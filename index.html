About Manka Liberator

Manka Liberator is an Author, Counselor, and dedicated Civil Society Worker with over 15 years of experience transforming lives.

A passionate advocate for the holistic well-being of women, youth, and children, she has served extensively across schools, churches, and private practice to champion mental health and spiritual growth.

She is the Founder and Executive Director of SAFE HOME (MHPSS) and the visionary behind LIBERTY GLOBAL ASSOCIATION.
